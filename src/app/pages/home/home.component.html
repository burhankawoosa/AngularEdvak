Practice Managment

Medical practice management software aids in the administration of day-to-day operations in a medical office. It acts as an intermediary between some forms of clinical activity, such as the reporting of diagnostic and treatment codes, and other types of work, including as arranging patient visits, validating insurance, and handling medical billing responsibilities.

Eelectronic Health Record

Edvak is focused on delivering EHR software that includes a wide range of features and functions to fulfil the demands of occupational and environmental medicine practitioners in both internal and external clinics. Patient Portal

Patients can view their health data released by their healthcare facility through our EHR patient portal. They can book in-person or virtual doctor's appointments online.
