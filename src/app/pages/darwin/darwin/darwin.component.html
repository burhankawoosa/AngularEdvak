Improving Patient Experience

Assistance for Patients: Darwin assist patients in getting real-time answers to their queries concerning health symptoms, different illnesses, possible medicine side effects, accessible treatment options, drug availability, and therapeutic tips.

Real-time Medication Reminders: for patients, especially older adults, can set timely reminders for taking medications on time without any miss.

Virtual Care: Patients can book an appointment with their preferred doctor just by conversing with Darwin. They will also get the option to upload their reports consistently if their medical issue requires it, without visiting the clinic or filling complex forms.
